Anna Goldenberg and the Lab

Overview

Anna Goldenberg is a Scientist in the Genetics and Genome Biology Program at SickKids Research Institute. She is also a Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science (status only) at University of Toronto (Computational Biology Group)

Professor Goldenberg's main research focus is to develop machine learning methods that can help to decipher human disease heterogeneity. This involves combining data from multiple heterogeneous sources while addressing missing data and noise, simultaneous subtyping and feature selection in very sparse settings and more. Her lab's contributions to machine learning include novel graph-based unsupervised feature selection methods and graphical models for subtyping in GWAS. They collaborate with clinicians to ensure that our work is relevant in the clinic.
